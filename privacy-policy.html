Privacy Policy

A to Z Packers & Movers — how we collect, use and protect your data

Effective date: 04 Oct 2025

A to Z Packers & Movers (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use our website, submit a lead form, contact us by phone/WhatsApp, or use our services; how we use and share that information; and the choices you have.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not submit your information.

Information We Collect

  • Lead form details: name, phone number, email, pickup & drop locations, service type, preferred date, and any message you provide.
  • Communications: call/WhatsApp/chat messages and related metadata (time, duration).
  • Website data: device info, IP address, pages viewed, and cookies/analytics (see Cookies section).
  • Billing/Service: addresses, inventory lists, and documents required to execute your move.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide quotes, schedule surveys, and deliver packing & moving services.
  • Respond to enquiries and offer customer support.
  • Improve our website, ads performance, and service quality.
  • Send service updates, invoices, and important notices.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or misuse.

Legal Bases (GDPR/Similar)

  • Consent – when you submit forms or opt-in to communication.
  • Contract – to provide quotes and fulfil moving services.
  • Legitimate interests – to run, secure, and improve our business.
  • Legal obligation – to keep records where required by law.

Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service partners (e.g., transport, insurance) strictly to complete your job.
  • Vendors for hosting, analytics, communications, and payments under data-processing terms.
  • Authorities if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above—typically 24 months after your last interaction, unless a longer period is required by law (e.g., tax and invoicing records).

Your Rights

Subject to local laws, you may:

  • Access, correct, update, or delete your information.
  • Object to or restrict processing; withdraw consent at any time.
  • Request data portability and lodge a complaint with your data authority.

To exercise rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies for website functionality, remembering preferences, measuring traffic, and improving ads. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not work without cookies.

Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; you share information at your own risk.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect their data.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your state/country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as contractual clauses with processors.
